HOW TO MAKE PORTUGUESE EGG TARTS

List of Ingredients:

  • 2 sheets of Frozen Puff Pastry
  • 260 g of Whole Milk
  • 60 g of Heavy Cream
  • 60 g of Sugar
  • 2 egg yolk
  • 1 tbsp of corn starch
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract

List of Tools:

  • 12 ct. cupcake/muffin pan
  • Weighing device
  • Whisk
  • Measuring spoons
  • Small cooking pot
  • Cooking/baking oil spray
  • Sandwich knife
  • Water cup with round rigid edge

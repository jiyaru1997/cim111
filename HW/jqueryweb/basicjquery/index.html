HOW TO MAKE PORTUGUESE EGG TARTS

List of Ingredients:

  • 2 sheets of Frozen Puff Pastry
  • 100g of Whole Milk
  • 140g of Heavy Cream
  • 40g of Sugar
  • 3 egg yolk
  • 10g corn starch

List of Tools:

  • 12 ct. cupcake/muffin pan
  • Weighing device
  • Whisk
  • Small cooking pot
  • Mesh strainer

Step 1: Prepare the Ingredients

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Prepare a box of Puff Pastry Sheet. Make the Puff Pastry Sheets into 12 dough balls.

Step 2: Mold Pastry

Flatten the balls into small rounds and fit them well into the muffin pan by pressing firmly on bottom and side. Repeat for remaining dough pieces. Or, you may use a cup to mark the pastry and cut the circular pastry using a sandwich knife.

Step 3: Cook Egg Custard

Heat the milk, heavy cream and sugar in the cooking pot until fully mixed. Add the egg yolks and flour in the mixed ingredients and beat together until light and foamy; strain the filling through a fine mesh strainer into a clean container.

Step 4: Ready to Bake

Spoon each crust about 80 percent full of the egg yolk mixture. You can also put rasin in the bottom of the crust based on your preference.

Step 5: Baking Process

Bake in the preheated oven until the crusts are golden brown, the filling is set, and the tarts are lightly browned, 15 to 20 minutes.
